# Restaurant Policies

## Cancellation Policy
Reservations can be cancelled up to 2 hours before the scheduled time. Late cancellations may incur a $10 fee.

## Refund Policy
Refunds are available within 24 hours of purchase with a valid receipt. Food quality issues are eligible for a full refund or replacement.

## Group Reservations
Parties of 8 or more require a deposit of $10 per person. The deposit is non-refundable if cancelled less than 24 hours before.

## Takeaway Policy
Orders must be picked up within 30 minutes of the ready time. Unpicked orders after 1 hour will be cancelled and charged full price.

## Payment Policy
We accept all major credit cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover. Cash payments are accepted for dine-in and takeaway. No personal checks accepted.

## Delivery Policy
Delivery is available within a 5-mile radius. Minimum order of $15 is required for delivery. Delivery fee is $3 and free for orders above $50.

## Allergy Policy
Customers with severe allergies should inform staff before ordering. We cannot guarantee cross-contamination-free preparation.
