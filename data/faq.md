# Frequently Asked Questions

## What are your hours?
We are open Monday to Friday 11am-10pm, Saturday 10am-11pm, Sunday 10am-9pm.

## Where are you located?
123 Main Street, Downtown, New York, NY 10001.

## Do you offer delivery?
Yes, we deliver within a 5-mile radius. Delivery fee is $3.

## Is parking available?
Yes, we have a free parking lot behind the restaurant.

## Do you have vegetarian options?
Yes, we have salads, veggie pizza, and pasta options.

## Do you have vegan options?
Yes, our salads and some pizzas can be made vegan on request.

## Can I bring my own cake?
Yes, you can bring your own cake for special occasions. A $5 plating fee applies.

## Is the restaurant kid-friendly?
Yes, we welcome families and have a kids menu available.

## Do you take walk-ins?
Yes, but we recommend making a reservation especially on weekends.

## Can I host a private event?
Yes, we have a private dining room for parties up to 30 people. Call us for pricing.
